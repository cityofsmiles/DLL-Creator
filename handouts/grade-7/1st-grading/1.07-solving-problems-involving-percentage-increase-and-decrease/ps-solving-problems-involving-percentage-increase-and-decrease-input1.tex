\noindent\textbf{Practice Exercises 1.07}

%\vspace{0.75ex}
\vspace{0.2ex}

Solve each problem.
\begin{enumerate}
    \item The price of a book increased from \pesos 200 to \pesos 250. Find the percentage increase.
    \item A store reduced the price of a shirt from \pesos 500 to \pesos 400. Find the percentage decrease.
    \item A school had 800 students last year, and this year it has 920 students. Find the percentage increase.
    \item The population of a town decreased from 15,000 to 13,500. Find the percentage decrease.
    \item A worker's salary increased from \pesos 12,000 to \pesos 15,000. Find the percentage increase.
    \item A laptop originally priced at \pesos 40,000 is now on sale for \pesos 36,000. Find the percentage decrease.
    \item A company’s revenue increased from \pesos 1,200,000 to \pesos 1,500,000. Find the percentage increase.
    \item A school's passing rate decreased from 85\% to 78\%. Find the percentage decrease.
    \item The weight of a watermelon increased from 2.5 kg to 3.2 kg. Find the percentage increase.
    \item A car’s value depreciated from \pesos 900,000 to \pesos 720,000. Find the percentage decrease.
\end{enumerate}
