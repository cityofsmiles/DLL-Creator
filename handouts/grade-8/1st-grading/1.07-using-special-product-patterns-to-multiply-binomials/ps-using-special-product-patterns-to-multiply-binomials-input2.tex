\noindent\textbf{Activity 1.7}

\vspace{0.75ex}

Use the special product patterns to simplify the following expressions.
\begin{multicols}{2}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \( (x + 4)^2 \)
    \item \( (y - 2)^2 \)
    \item \( (3x + 5)^2 \)
    \item \( (2y - 6)^2 \)
    \item \( (a + 7)^2 \)
    \item \( (m - 9)^2 \)
    \item \( (x + 3)(x - 3) \)
    \item \( (3y + 2)(3y - 2) \)
    \item \( (4a + 1)(4a - 1) \)
    \item \( (5m + 6)(5m - 6) \)
\end{enumerate}
\end{multicols}
